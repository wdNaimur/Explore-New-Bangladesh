Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Bangladesh can become a beacon of hope for the world, demonstrating how a country can rise from poverty through innovation, entrepreneurship, and the empowerment of its people. We must build a future where no one is left behind, where poverty becomes a term of the past, and where young minds are free to dream and create without limits. By focusing on social businesses, sustainable development, and the untapped potential of our youth, we can craft a Bangladesh that is not only self-reliant but also a global leader in solving humanity’s most pressing challenges. The future of Bangladesh lies in the hands of its people, and together, we can make the impossible, possible.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
